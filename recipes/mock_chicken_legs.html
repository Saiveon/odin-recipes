Mock Chicken Legs

Recipes.net, "Pork MOCK CHICKEN LEGS (CITY CHICKEN) | Recipes.net", 2022, via Youtube.com

Perfectly seasonsed and crispy fried mock chicken legs; they are made with veal and pork then browned in a pan of oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground veal
  • 1/4 pound ground pork
  • 1/4 cup crushed pineapple, well drained
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or as needed
